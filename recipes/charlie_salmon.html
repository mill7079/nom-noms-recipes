Charlie's Salmon Seasoning

Total time: 40 minutes | Yields: enough for 4 salmons

Ingredients

  • 3 teaspoons paprika
  • 4 teaspoons dry thyme
  • 2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dry oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 teaspoons white sugar

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small jar with enough space to shake/blend the spices thoroughly. Coat chicken or fish with canola or olive oil and apply the seasoning mix. Then, bake for about 15 minutes at 350 degrees (if using salmon).
