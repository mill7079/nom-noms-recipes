Pizza Dough

Prep time: 43 minutes | Yield: 2 pizzas

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 5 cups flour, divided
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil (2 per pan)

Preparation

  1. Mix warm water and yeast and let sit 5 minutes until yeast proofs.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  3. Add 2.5 cups flour to proofed yeast and mix well.
  4. Add additional 2.5 cups flour and salt. Knead until smooth and let rest in a covered bowl for 10 to 20 minutes.
  5. Pour 2 tablespoons of olive oil onto baking sheet or pizza pan and evenly coat bottom.
  6. Remove dough from bowl and add more flour as needed (a little at a time) to alleviate the stickiness fo the dough (usually a few tablespoons).
  7. Split dough in half, roll each ball of dough very thin, and place on greased pan.
  8. Top your pizza, leaving at least a 3/4-inch space on the edge for crust.
  9. Bake each pizza separately for 18-20 minutes (or 15 minutes for breadsticks).
