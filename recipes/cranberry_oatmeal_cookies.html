Chewy Cranberry-Oatmeal Cookies

Total time: ? minutes | Yield: ~44 cookies | Oven temp: 350F

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 1/2 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, slightly softened
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 1/3 cups (6 oz) dried cranberries

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; stir in the oats.
  3. With an electric mixer, beat the butter and both sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then scrape down the sides of the bowl.
  5. Add the honey and vanilla and beat until blended.
  6. Add the flour mixture in two additions, beating until well combined.
  7. Stir in the cranberries.
  8. Drop dough by heaping tablespoonful about 2 inches apart onto cookie sheets.
  9. Bake until the centers of the cookies are soft and no longer look wet (about 9-11 minutes); let cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks.
