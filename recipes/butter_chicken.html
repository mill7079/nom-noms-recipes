Butter Chicken

Total time: 30 minutes | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoon unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 pounds chicken breast, skinless, boneless, and diced into bite-sized pieces
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 inches ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 17 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoon fresh cilantro, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a frying pan, heat vegetable oil and melt 1 tablespoon of butter. Brown the diced chicken breast until golden. Move the chicken to one side, and melt the rest of the butter.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute). Add all of the spices and the salt, stir and cook for a minute then add the tomato sauce.
  3. Give everything a mix, cover with a lid and simmer on medium heat for 5-10 minutes for the chicken to cook and the flavors to settle.
  4. Carefully open the lid, stir in the cream, and allow the sauce to reduce a little. Serve warm over rice with chopped cilantro.
