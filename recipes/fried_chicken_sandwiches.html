Fried Chicken Sandwiches

Total time: 1.5 hours | Serves: 2

Ingredients for Chicken

  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon confectioner's sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • dash cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 jar of pickle juice + pickles
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg
  • canola oil for frying (about a cup)
  • sliced cheese, for serving

Preparation

  1. Put chicken breasts in pickle juice and let it marinate for an hour.
  2. Combine paprika, pepper, salt, confectioner’s sugar, flour, cayenne, garlic powder, and basil in a shallow dish.
  3. Whisk heavy cream and egg together in another shallow dish
  4. Heat oil in pan over medium-high heat (there should be enough oil to submerge the chicken half way). Then, place each chicken breast first in the egg mixture, then in the flour mixture, and finally in the oil. (***don’t double bread, the breading just falls off***) Fry it for 3 to 5 minutes before flipping it and frying for another 3 to 5 minutes. Serve on a bun with pickles, cheese, and sauce.
