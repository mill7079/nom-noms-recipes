Egg Drop Soup

Total time: 15 minutes | Yield: 2 bowls

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups chicken stock
  • 1/3 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or more :))
  • pinch white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1-2 eggs, lightly beaten

Preparation

  1. Bring the chicken stock, sesame oil, salt, and white pepper to a simmer in a medium soup pot. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  2. While the soup is coming to a simmer, combine the cornstarch with enough water to form a slurry. Make sure this slurry is mixed well. Once the soup is simmering, slowly pour the slurry into the soup, stirring continuously to avoid clumps of cooked starch.
  3. Once thickened, pour in the egg, stirring continuously and quickly to form long swirls of egg. Pour into bowls and serve.
