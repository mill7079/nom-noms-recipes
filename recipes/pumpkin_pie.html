Pumpkin Pie

Total time: 1.5 hours | Yield: 1 pie

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs plus the yolk of a third egg
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 15-oz can pumpkin purée
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream or one 12 oz can evaporated milk
  • 1 pie crust, chilled or frozen

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Mix in the brown sugar, white sugar, salt, and spices. Mix in the pumpkin purée. Stir in the cream. Beat together until everything is well-mixed.
  3. Pour the filling into an uncooked chilled or frozen pie shell. Bake at a high temperature of 425 degrees F for 15 minutes. Then, lower the temperature to 350 degrees F. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes more. The pie is done when a knife tip inserted in the center comes out wet but relatively clean. The center should be just barely jiggly.
