Pfeffernüsse

Total time: ~3 hours | Yield: 3 dozen cookies | Oven Temp: 325F

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons anise extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup powdered sugar for dusting

Preparation

  1. Stir together the molasses, honey, shortening, and margarine in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until creamy. Remove from heat, cool to room temperature, and stir in the eggs.
  2. Combine the flour, white sugar, brown sugar, cardamom, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, anise, cinnamon, baking soda, pepper, and salt in a large bowl. Add the molasses mixture and stir until thoroughly combined.
  3. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit. Roll the dough into acorn-sized balls, arranging 1 inch apart on baking sheets.
  5. Bake in preheated oven 10-15 minutes. Move to a rack to cool; dust cooled cookies with powdered sugar.
