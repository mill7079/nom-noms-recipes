Lecso

Total time: 45 minutes | Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 6 large unpeeled new potatoes, cubed
  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 8-ounce can stewed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 12-ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup water

Preparation

  1. Brown sausage in a saucepan.
  2. In a large pot over medium heat, cook potatoes in butter with garlic, stirring frequently and not allowing the garlic to burn. Stir in onion, bell pepper, sausage, tomatoes, spaghetti sauce, paprika, and water. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until potatoes are just tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
