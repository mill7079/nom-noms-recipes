Champurrado (Chocolate Atole)

Total time: 30 minutes | Yield: 8

Ingredients

  • 6 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup masa harina (corn flour)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 3 ounces grated unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Preparation

  1. Heat the milk and grated chocolate in a saucepan, stirring to dissolve the chocolate.
  2. When chocolate is completely dissolved, remove from th eheat and set aside to keep warm.
  3. In another pan, mix the masa harina with the water and place over low heat. Add the cinnamon stick and cookk until the mixture has thickened and the masa becomes translucent.
  4. Add the chocolate milk and usgar. Stir to dissolve the sugar and simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Remove the cinnamon stick and serve hot in cups or mugs.
