Crispy Cuban Tofu Rice and Beans

Total time: 1 hour | Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 package firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 15 oz can of black beans, drained
  • 2 cups pre cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon fresh oregano for garnish

Preparation

  1. Drain tofu well, and squeeze water from tofu with towel. Slice tofu into strips. Add oils to the pan and place tofu strips in pan on medium high heat.
  2. Mix all spices together in a cup. Sprinkle 1/2 of the spices on tofu in pan. Cook tofu for approximately 5 minutes on each side until you get the crispy effect that you want. Remove tofu from pan when cooked.
  3. Add onions, garlic, rice, tomatoes, and beans to pan with additional oil if needed. Saut√© on medium heat until onions are tender and ingredients are heated, approximately 6 minutes.
  4. Remove rice bean mix from pan and place in servings bowls. Top with tofu strips. Garnish with fresh oregano.
