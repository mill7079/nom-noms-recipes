Black Bean Tortas with Coconut Chipotle Mayo

Total time: 1 hour | Serves: 4

Ingredients for Mayo

  • 1/2 cup canned coconut milk
  • 5 tablespoons chipotle hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon ground chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • pinch of salt

Ingredients for Black Beans

  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Juice of 1 lime

ingredients to Top With:

  • 4 crusty rolls, split and toasted
  • sliced tomatoes
  • sliced avocado
  • lettuce

Preparation

  1. First, make the mayo. Throw all those mayo ingredients in your blender or food processor and run that motherfucker on high for about a minute so everything is good and mixed. Taste and see if you want more hot sauce. Pour this all in a cup or bowl and store it in the fridge until you need it. It will thicken in there, just fucking be patient.
  2. Next, make the beans. Heat the oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Throw in the onion and sauté it until it starts to look golden brown, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, chili powder, and cumin and cook for another 30 seconds. Add the beans and broth and lime and stir that shit up. Let it come to a simmer and then turn down the heat real low. Cook for a little bit until it’s thicker. Using a potato masher or big-ass spoon or fork, smash all those beans best you can. Think chunky guacamole. Add some more salt or spices if that’s the kinda shit you’re into. Now turn off the heat and make a torta.
  3. Grab a toasted roll and smear with a bunch of the coconut chipotle mayo. Pile a fuckton of beans on the bottom half. In between, add whatever the fuck you want. Lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, and some avocado are the time-tested choices, but be creative and shit. Serve right away with some extra hot sauce.
