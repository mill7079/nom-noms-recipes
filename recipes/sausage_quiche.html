Sausage Quiche

Total time: 1 hour | Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch unbaked deep dish frozen pie crust
  • 8 ounce mild or spicy breakfast sausage
  • 5 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
  • hot sauce (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Place pie shell on cookie sheet. Set aside.
  2. In a non-stick skillet over medium high heat, cook the sausage until no pink remains. Add the green onion and cook for one minute longer. Drain excess fat from pan.
  3. Spread ½ of the sausage mixture in the pie shell and sprinkle with ½ of the cheese. Spread remaining sausage over the cheese. In a medium size mixing bowl whisk together the half and half, eggs, salt, pepper, and mustard. Add a few dashes of hot sauce, if desired. Pour evenly over the sausage and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  4. Place into oven and bake for 45-50 minutes or until the center is set when gently shaken. Check at 30 minutes and lay a piece of aluminum foil on top to prevent over browning if needed. Allow to rest for 30 minutes and serve warm.
