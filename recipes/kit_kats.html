Homemade Kit Kat Candy Bars

Total time: ? | Yield: ?

Ingredients

  • 1 box square butter crackers (e.g. Club crackers)
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 1/4 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cup butterscotch chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips

Preparation

  1. Spray a baking pan with cooking oil (or line with parchment, I believe) and lay crackers edge-to-edge in a single layer along the bottom.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan and stir in the milk.
  3. Add both sugars and bring to a boil.
  4. Stir in the graham cracker crumbs and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Pour about half the filling over the crackers. Make another layer of crackers, pour the rest of the filling on top, and finish with a third layer of crackers. Cover in cling wrap and put in fridge to firm up.
  6. Melt the chips together in a double boiler. Once integrated (the butterscotch chips are especially fussy), pour the chocolate evenly over the cracker layers.
  7. Let set and cut into pieces.
