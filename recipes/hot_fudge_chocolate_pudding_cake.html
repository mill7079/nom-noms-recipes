Hot Fudge Chocolate Pudding Cake

Total time: 40 minutes | Yield: 9

Ingredients for Cake

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Ingredients for Sauce

  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 1/4 cups boiling water

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 9-inch square baking pan. Pre-boil water in a kettle while preparing the cake.
  2. (Cake) Combine 1/2 cup granulated sugar with the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Add in the milk, butter, and vanilla; whisk until smooth and fairly lump free. Pour batter into prepared baking pan or dish and spread evenly.
  3. (Sauce) Combine together the remaining 1/2 cup white sugar, brown sugar, and remaining 3 tablespoons cocoa powder. Evenly sprinkle mixture over the batter. Slowly pour the boiling water over the top (DO NOT STIR IN).
  4. Bake 35 minutes or until the center is almost set. Remove from oven. Allow to cool for 15 minutes.
