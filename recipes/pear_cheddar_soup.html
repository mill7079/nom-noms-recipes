Pear Cheddar Soup

Total time: 40 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 3 medium ripe pears, peeled and chopped (the more the merrier)
  • 2-3 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • fresh pear slices for topping, optional

Preparation

  1. In a large pot, heat butter over medium-high heat; saut√© onion and garlic until tender (7-9 minutes). Stir in flour and paprika until blended, cook and stir 2 minutes. Gradually stir in broth. Add chopped pears, bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until pears are tender (about 15 minutes). Stir occasionally.
  2. Puree soup using an immersion blender or cool slightly and puree soup in batches in a blender; return to pan. Add the cheese and pepper; cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted, 3-5 minutes. If desired, top with pear slices.
