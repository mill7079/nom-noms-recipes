Cheese Enchiladas

Total time: 50 minutes | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 8 tablespoons flour
  • 6 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • corn tortillas (approx. 15)
  • Mexican cheese (1 or 2 bags)

Preparation

  1. Add oil to pot and heat on medium. Pour in flour and whisk together and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and oregano and mix until clumpy. Pour in chicken broth, whisking the entire time and until there are no more clumps. Heat for 15 minutes or until thickened.
  3. After you have made your sauce, you will want to dip your corn tortillas in the sauce until they are soft and immediately put them into a greased 11x7 pan. From there you will add cheese, roll it up, and push it to the end of the pan.
  4. Continue doing this with your tortillas until your pan is full. Pour the excess sauce over your tortillas. Sprinkle the top with more cheese.
  5. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
