Pizza Crust

Prep time: 30 minutes | Yield: 1 12-inch pizza crust

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes. Then stir in flour, salt, and oil. Beat until smooth.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F, and let dough rest while oven preheats.
  3. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round. Transfer crust to a lightly greased pizza pan dusted with cornmeal. Spread with desired toppings and bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
