Mango Sticky Rice

Total time: 3 hours to overnight | Servings: 5

Ingredients

  • Fresh mangoes or other fruit
  • 1 cup glutinous/sweet rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk (I use the entire can and adjust other sauce amounts accordingly)
  • 1 tablespoon coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon corn starch
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Soak rice for 2 hours, or up to overnight. Drain water when ready.
  2. Using a steamer or double boiler, cook the rice for 20-30 minutes. If the rice is soft and not hard in the middle, then it is done. While the rice cooks, slices mangoes.
  3. Prepare the sweet coconut sauce by bringing 1 cup of coconut milk, sugar, and salt to a boil. Once it reaches a boil, pour half the sauce over the cooked rice.
  4. Combine 1 tablespoon of coconut milk and corn starch, then add that into the pot to thicken the sweet coconut sauce. Boil for a few minutes until sauce is thickened.
  5. Let cool for 15-20 minutes. Serve rice with mangoes and drizzle thick sweet coconut sauce on top. Garnish with sesame seeds.
